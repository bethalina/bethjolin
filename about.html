About me

Hello, I'm Beth

I love creating engaging, simple and inspirational design solutions.

My work combines a strong graphic design background, oodles of research and an uncanny intuitive ability to narrate my clients ideas successfully whatever the medium.

I believe that great design solutions are the result of a fluid collaboration between a team of individuals, and innumerable failed prototypes. Designer, client, and users must all take a seat at the table to ensure a valuable product. I find inspiration in my everyday things from a carefully crafted beer label, to the patterns found on the back of a Red Bellied Woodpecker.

When not immersed in a design project you will find me stalking wildlife, particularly those that are feathered. Nature and meditation are how I create balance in my life, and where I find inspiration. I am especially passionate and curious about our own innate ability to heal ourselves. I am an avid walker, runner, bicyclist, and swimmer. I also enjoy cooking locally sourced meals for family and friends.
